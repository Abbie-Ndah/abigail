    Current Research

    Efficacy of Neem (Azadirachta indica) Leaf Extract Against Fungi Pathogen Associated with Watermelon (Citrullus lanatus) Spoilage

    Advisor

    Dr. Bashir Ali

    To ascertain the effectiveness of Azadirachta indica leaf extract on pathogen , we had to isolate, purify and determine growth rate, zone of inhibition and colony count of fungi pathogen of watermelon using standard mycological techniques.     Undergraduate Research

    Prevalence of Fungi Diseases of Melon (Cucumeropsis mannii) in Dekina Local Government of Kogi State.

    Advisor

    Dr. Bahir Ali

    I conducted research under the mentorship of Dr. Bahir Ali in Biological Science General lab at Kogi State University in Nigeria from October 2020 – April 2021.

    The aim was evaluating prevalent fungi disease of melon. This was because of the low yield of melon in Dekina local government of Kogi State and no research has been done on it in Kogi State. Melon samples were gotten from the study area. The pathogenic fungi were isolated from the melon by explanting the spoilt melon obtained aseptically unto potato dextrose agar (PDA) supplemented with streptomycin. The developing isolate were purified by repeated sub culture and identified microscopically using the slide culture technique. The research shows that Rhizopus stolonifer recorded the highest prevalence among the fungal pathogens isolated, which could be as a result of the environmental conditions in the sample areas such as high temperature, relative humidity and soil conditions that favor the growth and sporulation of Rhizopus sp.
